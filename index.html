The History of Angel Island

China is currently a significant force in the world's economy. As a result, there is a very high chance that at least one thing that was made in China is in your house or building. In 2020, China's population consisted of 1.402 billion people, making it the world's largest country. Additionally, China is the fourth-largest country in land area at 9,596,960 square kilometers. As China is such a large country, its climate and terrain vary, ranging from cold in the northern areas to more tropical temperatures in the south. China's terrain is mostly mountains in the west and primarily plains and more hilly areas in the east. China's official name is the People's Republic of China, The English name for China comes from the Qin dynasty, and the Chinese name means Middle Country. The capital of China is Beijing. China has been a communist party-led state since October 1st, 1949, and the president has been Xi Jinping since 2013. China's GDP in 2020 was about $23 trillion, the GDP growth rate was about 6%, and the average GDP per capita was about $16,400 in 2020.
